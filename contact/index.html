Frequently Asked

How quickly will I get a response?
General inquiries are answered within one business day. Data requests and research partnerships may take 3–5 business days depending on complexity.
How do I request a custom dataset?
Use the form with topic "Data Request" or email [email protected] directly. Specify the disease, date range, geographic scope, and intended use. Requests may require a data use agreement.
Is the dashboard data publicly available?
Yes — aggregated surveillance data is freely available via the Data Explorer page. Individual-level records are restricted and require a formal data request and DUA.
Who should I contact to report a disease?
Reportable disease cases must be reported directly to NDDoH via the mandatory reporting line at (701) 328-3019. Do not use this contact form for disease reporting.
